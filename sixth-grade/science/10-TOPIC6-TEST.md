### Section E: Short Answer / Essay

**18.** (4 points) Precipitation is most likely on **Wednesday**. On Wednesday, the air temperature and the dew point are both 70 degrees F, which means the relative humidity is 100%. When relative humidity reaches 100%, the air is completely saturated and cannot hold any more water vapor. At this point, condensation occurs and precipitation is very likely. On Monday and Tuesday, the dew point is still below the air temperature, so the air can still hold more moisture.

**Scoring:** Award 1 point for identifying Wednesday. Award 1 point for noting that the temperature equals the dew point. Award 1 point for mentioning 100% relative humidity means the air is saturated. Award 1 point for explaining that saturation leads to condensation and precipitation.

**19.** (3 points) Transpiration is the process by which plants release water vapor through tiny pores in their leaves. This water vapor rises into the atmosphere and contributes to the water cycle by adding moisture to the air, which can then condense to form clouds and eventually fall as precipitation.

**Scoring:** Award 1 point for defining transpiration (plants releasing water vapor). Award 1 point for mentioning it happens through leaves. Award 1 point for explaining how it connects to the water cycle (adds moisture to air / contributes to cloud formation).
