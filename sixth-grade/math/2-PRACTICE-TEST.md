### Section A: Number Sense & Operations

**1.** −7, −3, −1, 0, 2, 5

**2.** 180 = 2² × 3² × 5
   (180 = 2 × 90 = 2 × 2 × 45 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 15 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 5)

**3.** 3 + 4 × (4)² ÷ 8 = 3 + 4 × 16 ÷ 8 = 3 + 64 ÷ 8 = 3 + 8 = **11**

**4.** (−8) + (+5) = −3; (−3) + (−3) = −6; (−6) + (+9) = **+3**

**5.** (−6)(−4) = +24; (+24) ÷ (−3) = **−8**

**6.** 64 ÷ 8 = 8; 8 × 3 = **24**

**7.** Let x = 0.363636...
   100x = 36.363636...
   100x − x = 36
   99x = 36
   x = 36/99 = **4/11**

**8.** √15 is **irrational**. 15 is not a perfect square, so its square root is a non-terminating, non-repeating decimal.
